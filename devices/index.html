    My devices

    Redmi Note 8 (ginkgo)

    Main phone. Has ArrowOS 11, fastest ROM for this device, installed. Doesn't have any GApps, because it does much useless stuff at the background.

    Fun fact

    I've hard-bricked it when flashed too old version of MIUI and had to repair it through EDL.

    PC (potato)

    Main PC with ancient parts. Intel i3-2100, NVIDIA GT440, 8GB of RAM. Works smooth for me, because I haven't tried anything better. However, Linux sucks on it, because of ancient NVIDIA graphics card. But Windows works just fine (Windows 7 is fastest for it, but it's too old).

    Fun fact

    After two years, SSD still lives, even after reinstalling OSes every two weeks, because I'm getting bored.

    Redmi 7A (pine)

    Second phone. Using it as a hotspot, a way to use apps for normies (e.g. banking, WhatsApp, school stuff) and just a fallback, when something will go wrong on ginkgo. Yes, I will take two phones when I need to go somewhere, or I'll just leave my ginkgo at home.

    Fun fact

    Sometimes it randomly reboots pretending that its EMMC is about to die.
