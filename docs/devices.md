# My devices

## Redmi Note 8 (ginkgo)

Main phone. Has ArrowOS 12 installed. Doesn't have any GApps, because it does much useless stuff at the background.
!!! info "Fun facts"
    - I've hard-bricked it when flashed too old version of MIUI and had to repair it through EDL.
    - Instead of shutting down it just reboots on the first time, and `adb reboot recovery` will actually reboot into system on the first time too.

## PC (potato)

Main PC with ancient parts. Intel i3-2100, NVIDIA GT440, 8GB of RAM. Works smooth for me, because I haven't tried anything better. However, when it comes to Minecraft, Linux sucks, because of ancient NVIDIA graphics card. But on Windows works just fine (Windows 7 is fastest for it, but it's too old).
!!! info "Fun fact"
    After three years, SSD still lives, even after reinstalling OSes every two weeks, because I'm getting bored.

## Redmi 7A (pine)

Second phone. Using it as a hotspot, a way to use apps for normies (e.g. banking, WhatsApp and other gov. stuff) and just a fallback, when something goes wrong on ginkgo. Yes, I'll take two phones when I need to go somewhere, or I'll just leave my ginkgo at home.
!!! info "Fun fact"
